What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity focuses on protecting systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage. It includes risk management, security policies, monitoring, and prevention.

What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking is the practice of legally breaking into systems to identify vulnerabilities before malicious hackers exploit them. Ethical hackers use penetration testing, social engineering, and security tools to strengthen defenses.

Why are both important?

Cybersecurity builds the walls.

Ethical hacking tests the walls.

Together, they create a complete security ecosystem.
